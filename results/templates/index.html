What is Warwick CATulator?

Warwick CATulator is an online tool to help you track your progress in your univeristy modules. Warwick Catulator can:

  • Track results for all of your module assessment
  • Automatically calculate module and year grades using the correct weightings
  • Let you find out the assessment grades you need to acheive any module result.

How do I use Warwick CATulator?

Simply login with your Warwick ITS account. We then get your course infomation to automatically setup your account so that all you have to do is input assessment results. To get started, click below...
