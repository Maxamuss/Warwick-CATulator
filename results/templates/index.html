What is Warwick CATulator?

Warwick CATulator is an online tool to help you track your progress in your university modules. It can:

  • Track results for all of your module's assessments
  • Automatically calculate module and year grades using the correct weightings
  • Let you find out the assessment grades you need to acheive any module result
  • See how you are assessed in each module.
All this in one place.

How do I use Warwick CATulator?

Simply login with your Warwick ITS account and sit back. We get your course information to automatically setup your profile so that all you have to do is input assessment results. To get started, click below.
